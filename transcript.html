FAI Grassroots Coach Education Program Transcript

00:02 Welcome to the Football Association of Ireland's Grassroots coach education program. This video helps you understand how to enter and progress through its Core Qualifications and stages up to and including the UEFA C license. All of our FAI coach education courses are based on the UEFA coaching convention and aligned with the FAI Player Development and are designed to promote key approaches to development and learning.

00:34 You will need to find the right entry point into the program, matching your experience and involvement. Entry point one is a starting point for most volunteer and parent coaches, developing fundamentals for coaching younger players. Our BDP-1 course focuses on coaching six to nine-year-olds, followed by PDP2 for ages 10-12. Coaches with experience in the 13-17 range...

01:14 Entry point two, with the PDP3 course, builds expertise and age-appropriate skills for experienced coaches. If you coach adult amateurs, entry point three with the adult amateur license may be ideal. Each path results in a Grassroots certificate, making you eligible for a Grassroots coach ID card.

01:52 - As part of the FAI's commitment to lifelong learning, your certificate remains valid for a minimum of three years. That time can be extended through earning a further 15 CPD credits in that three-year period. CPD credits can be accumulated by completing further workshops and courses or through attending FAI coaching conferences...

02:26 - These courses can also provide valuable CPD credits for those looking to maintain qualifications and enhance future higher-level coaching qualifications. Once you've achieved your entry-level certificate, the FAI offers a whole range of workshops that will enhance and develop your expertise. These act as a valuable source of CPD credits and are the gatekeeper to the next qualification, the national D license...

03:02 - If you want to take this step, you will need to join the FAI licensed coaches club and register with the FAI coach education learning management system, opening up access to a whole host of resources, FAI sessions planning software, and opportunities to earn CPD credits online...

03:40 With a minimum of five years coaching experience, you can advance toward the UEFA Leaders Award, aligned with the FAI Club Mark program. Completing the Leaders Award leads to the UEFA C license, the pinnacle qualification within the Grassroots program.
